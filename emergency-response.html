Emergency Response Procedures

SFAF has implemented the following measures to handle workplace violence emergencies:

  • AlertMedia ▶
    SFAF uses AlertMedia to contact employees in the event of workplace violence emergencies to check the health and safety of employees and to communicate orders, response plans, or updates.
  • Evacuation and Shelter-in-Place Plans ▶
    SFAF maintains and trains employees in evacuation and shelter-in-place plans at all our sites. Appropriate emergency evacuation signage is posted at all sites.
  • Emergency Buttons ▶
    Group and Counseling rooms at 940 Howard and 470 Castro are equipped with emergency buttons that notify all staff of an emergency and its location. Reception desks at 940 Howard, 470 Castro, and 117 6th Street are each equipped with silent alarm buttons that call the police.
