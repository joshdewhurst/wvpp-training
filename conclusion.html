Conclusion

The San Francisco AIDS Foundation (SFAF) is dedicated to ensuring a safe and healthy work environment for all employees. Our Workplace Violence Prevention Plan (WVPP) is a comprehensive approach to addressing and preventing incidents of workplace violence.

By adhering to the WVPP, participating in training, and actively engaging in workplace safety practices, employees contribute to a respectful and secure work environment.

Key Takeaways:

  • Understanding and identifying different types of workplace violence.
  • The importance of reporting incidents and the procedures to do so.
  • The roles and responsibilities of employees and management in preventing workplace violence.

Together, we can maintain a safe and supportive workplace where everyone feels valued and protected.
