Workplace Violence Incident Reporting Procedure

To ensure effective handling of workplace violence incidents, the following procedures are implemented:

▶ 1. Reporting threats or acts of workplace violence
Employees should report any threats or acts of workplace violence to their supervisor or manager. Supervisors will then inform the WVPP administrator/Safety Officer. Reports can be made anonymously through the Healthicity platform.
▶ 2. Alternative reporting methods
If anonymous reporting is not feasible or preferred, employees can report incidents directly to their management or supervisor, or contact People & Culture at peopleandculture@sfaf.org.
▶ 3. Non-retaliation policy
SFAF has a strict non-retaliation policy. Any instances of retaliation are dealt with swiftly and decisively to ensure a safe reporting environment.
