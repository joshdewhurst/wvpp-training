Workplace Violence Incident Reporting Procedure

SFAF will implement the following effective procedures to ensure that:

  • All threats or acts of workplace violence are reported to an employeeâ€™s supervisor or manager, who will inform the WVPP administrator/Safety Officer. This will be accomplished by utilizing the Healthicity platform for anonymous reporting.
  • If anonymous reporting is not feasible or preferred, employees can report incidents directly to their management or supervisor, or they can contact People & Culture through peopleandculture@sfaf.org. People & Culture will ensure that the WVPP administrator/Safety Officer are looped in to handle the incident accordingly.
  • A strict non-retaliation policy is in place, and any instances of retaliation are dealt with swiftly and decisively.
