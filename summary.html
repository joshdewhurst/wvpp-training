Summary

Throughout this training, we have covered essential aspects of the Workplace Violence Prevention Plan (WVPP) at SFAF. Here are the three key takeaways:

  • Comprehensive Understanding: Gain a thorough understanding of workplace violence types, incident reporting procedures, and emergency response protocols.
  • Active Participation: Engage actively in identifying, evaluating, and preventing workplace violence through proactive measures and adherence to WVPP guidelines.
  • Commitment to Safety: Recognize the importance of maintaining a safe and respectful work environment, and your role in supporting and upholding these standards.

Thank you for your participation and commitment to making SFAF a safer place for everyone.
