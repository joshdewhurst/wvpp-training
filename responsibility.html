Responsibility

The WVPP administrator, Nathaniel Anderson, and Julie Huang, Employee Relations Partner, have the authority and responsibility for implementing the provisions of this plan for SFAF.

See below for their contact information:

  • Nathaniel Anderson, Facilities Director, nanderson@sfaf.org
  • Julie Huang, Employee Relations Partner, jhuang@sfaf.org

All managers and supervisors are responsible for implementing and maintaining the WVPP in their work areas and for answering employee questions about the WVPP.

Responsible parties are tasked with responding to imminent hazards, documenting corrective actions, and finding corrective measures to address hazards.
